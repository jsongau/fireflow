Diff focus

One column of attributes. Each row shows both values and flags them as the same or different with an icon and a word, so the meaning never rides on colour alone. Toggle to only the rows that differ when you want the short answer.

Honesty notes. Allergens and preparation are read from the Multi pack statement and are bound to that exact format. Switch to a Big Bowl or Cup and the honest answer becomes verify the current package, because carrying one format's facts onto another would be wrong for a food product. Creaminess and first time fit are labelled editorial. Heat words are placed in a fixed order for the bars, which is a positioning ladder and not a Scoville reading.
