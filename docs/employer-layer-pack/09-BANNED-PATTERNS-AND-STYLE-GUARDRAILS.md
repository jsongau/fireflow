# Banned Patterns and Style Guardrails

## Banned words and phrases

Avoid:

- seamless
- robust
- leverage
- unlock
- revolutionize
- game-changing
- innovative solution
- cutting-edge
- next-generation
- holistic
- dynamic
- empower
- synergy
- frictionless journey
- not just X, but Y
- from the moment
- designed to delight
- powerful platform

## Punctuation rules

Avoid excessive em dashes.

Avoid arrows in visible CTAs.

Do not use:

- Learn more →
- Start →
- Next →

Use:

- Enter FireFlow
- Explore with Nathan
- View case timeline
- Continue exploring
- Open Order-to-Cash study

## Claim guardrails

Do not claim:

- direct SAP tenure
- SAP expert
- production SAP implementation
- Samyang internal access
- real Samyang CX data
- real operational metrics
- real customer cases
- official affiliation

## Tone guardrails

Do not make Nathan sound like:

- a corporate marketer
- a motivational speaker
- an overconfident consultant
- a chatbot
- a generic résumé writer

Make Nathan sound like:

- a customer operations trainer
- a practical SOP writer
- a systems thinker
- someone who knows what goes wrong when handoffs are unclear

## Copy smell test

Reject any line that could be copied into any SaaS website.

Good copy names the actual operating thing:

- order
- product
- customer record
- billing issue
- delivery status
- evidence
- owner
- escalation
- next update
- source label
- case history

Bad copy hides behind abstractions:

- experience
- value
- platform
- solution
- workflow optimization
- transformation
