# Role-to-Feature Evidence Map

## Purpose

Convert the site from “cool project” into “evidence of role readiness.”

This should be a visible employer-facing section or a mode-specific panel.

## Core evidence table

| Job requirement / role need | FireFlow feature evidence | What it proves | Nathan’s experience angle |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product knowledge | Product dossier, source labels, format-specific details | Can learn a large product catalog and explain it clearly | Consumer products and retail operations background |
| Customer communication | Consumer Care path, support status, case updates | Can structure answers and reduce repeat questions | Customer-facing issue resolution |
| Order management | Order-to-Cash Process Intelligence chapter | Understands the flow from order entry through billing and resolution | Operations mindset across intake, ownership, and follow up |
| SAP SD concepts | SAP SD aligned workflow study | Understands order entry, pricing, delivery, billing, customer records, and order status at a workflow level | Honest readiness to support and learn the system |
| Pricing exceptions | Pricing review / dispute logic | Recognizes that price mismatches become customer and finance problems if not surfaced early | Experience explaining costs and resolving discrepancies |
| Delivery coordination | Delivery status and order support flows | Can communicate what changed, who owns it, and when the next update is due | Handoff and expectation management |
| Billing inquiries | Deduction, invoice, and billing friction workflows | Can separate facts, evidence, and ownership | Revenue cycle and payment issue discipline |
| Customer master data | Customer / vendor profile and case context preservation | Understands why accurate records prevent downstream confusion | Front-office data accuracy and documentation |
| Escalation | Severity routing, specialist escalation, owner field | Can identify risk and route issues without overpromising | Calm issue handling |
| Continuous improvement | Product Signals section | Can turn repeated inquiries into training and process improvements | SOP and workflow design mindset |
| Training ability | Operator Notes and Nathan’s Read panels | Can teach systems in plain English | Strong fit for cross-functional communication |

## Short version for visible UI

Use this as a compact card section:

### What this demo is meant to prove

- I can learn a product catalog and explain it clearly.
- I can structure customer and vendor inquiries into routed workflows.
- I can preserve product, order, evidence, owner, and next-update context.
- I can think through Order-to-Cash friction honestly.
- I can turn repeated questions into training, SOPs, and process improvements.
- I can communicate systems in a way a team can actually use.

## Role connection copy

> FireFlow connects the same operating pieces a CX team has to hold together every day: product knowledge, order context, customer communication, vendor support, billing friction, escalation, ownership, and continuous improvement.

## What not to do

Do not turn this into a résumé wall.

Avoid:

- Long career chronology
- Every past employer
- Unsupported performance claims
- Exaggerated SAP claims
- “I am the perfect candidate” language

The evidence should come from the product, not from self praise.
