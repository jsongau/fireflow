# Employer-Facing Voice

## Goal

Help a hiring manager understand Nathan's experience, thinking, and fit without turning the product into a résumé wall.

## Positioning

Nathan is strongest when presented as a cross-functional business operator who connects customers, people, processes, systems, and revenue.

For FireFlow, emphasize:

- more than eight years across consumer products, retail customer operations, and customer-facing operations
- structured intake and information accuracy
- complex issue resolution
- billing and dispute handling
- process training and standardization
- customer communication
- escalation ownership
- cross-functional coordination
- continuous improvement
- systems thinking and practical automation

## Voice rules

- Lead with what Nathan understands and has done.
- Show evidence through the artifact.
- Do not repeat the job description as a checklist.
- Do not imply access to Samyang systems, data, employees, or customers.
- Do not claim direct SAP tenure that Nathan does not have.
- Make every claim interview-defensible.

## Preferred framing

> I built FireFlow to show how I think about customer operations when product knowledge, order status, billing, ownership, and communication need to work as one system.

> My background is in consumer products, retail operations, customer service, billing, and high-stakes issue resolution. FireFlow translates that experience into a structured operating model for order management and continuous improvement.

> This is an independent concept built from public product information and synthetic operational data. It demonstrates my approach, not access to Samyang's internal systems.

## Avoid

- perfect candidate
- uniquely qualified
- visionary leader
- world-class
- proven SAP expert
- transformed everything
- guaranteed results
- production-ready enterprise platform

## Résumé references

When linking to a résumé or experience page, use direct labels:

- View Nathan's experience
- Open Nathan's résumé
- Discuss the role

Do not use vague labels such as Discover more or Unlock my journey.
