# Banned AI Patterns

Remove or rewrite these patterns unless a direct quotation requires them.

## Banned words and phrases

- seamless
- robust
- leverage
- unlock
- revolutionize
- elevate your experience
- cutting-edge
- game-changing
- next-level
- world-class
- best-in-class
- innovative solution
- powerful platform
- dynamic ecosystem
- transformative journey
- effortlessly
- dive into
- embark on
- in today's fast-paced world
- at the end of the day
- not just X, but Y
- from X to Y as a dramatic opener
- where X meets Y
- designed to empower

## Banned structural habits

- em dashes used repeatedly to connect thoughts
- visible arrows used as sentence structure or CTA decoration
- a three-adjective stack before every noun
- several sentences that begin with This powerful, This innovative, or This seamless
- résumé claims without evidence
- fake quotes
- fake metrics
- fake urgency
- vague praise of the product
- repeating the same disclaimer in every section
- explaining obvious clicks
- presenting a feature list as a strategy
- ending every paragraph with a sales CTA

## Rewrite pattern

Replace abstract praise with the operating detail.

AI-like:

> FireFlow delivers a seamless, robust experience that empowers teams to unlock actionable insights.

Nathan-style:

> FireFlow keeps the product, case evidence, owner, and next customer update connected so the team can act without rebuilding the context.
